The task - Creation of a highly loaded information portal with a complex system of searching and structuring information. Automation of search from various sources and structuring of the search query in a convenient format.

Implementation-Since it requires the implementation of complex algorithms, so we decided to write from scratch its own libraries (PHP), also taking into account the specifics of the project, more than 50 different parsers were used to collect information. For dynamic, the front-end part is implemented on the Angular JS framework, Apache is selected as the web server.

As a result, specially created libraries in tandem with parsers provided a fast and accurate selection of information for each unique query.  And Angular on the client side allowed to significantly accelerate the work with the interface.

Purpose - The reporter is an internal project of our company. Before the team was tasked to create a corporate EMS system for accounting of working hours combining rich functionality and simplicity of the interface.

Given the objectives, the optimal technology for such a system are PHP frameworks capable of working with large data sets and high loads. In this case, it's Laravel and Angular on the client side.

Thanks to the flexibility of the technologies used, it was possible to implement a multi-stage time recording system, group project management tools, intelligent work calendar distribution (taking into account the staff load on peak dates, the choice of the optimal vacation interval), uniform load distribution for personnel in case of force majeure Project of one or several employees for any reason)

In the result, our team in the shortest time possible to implement a time-consuming project, which completely doubled the needs of the company. EMS includes all the necessary functionality and at the same time is not overloaded with unnecessary features, which we could not get from existing products on the market.
